Finance Division

Enhancing Production of Onions

Although B onion production is successfully done in the country we are yet importing to meet the national requirement Intension of the President is to strengthen the local agriculture and put an end to crop production imports. The President has entrusted this enormous responsibility to the Ministry of Agriculture. All arrangements have been finalized to bring 5000 hactares under B onion cultivation in 2021. 25000 kg of seed B onion is required for implementing this under taking.

Pasion Fruit Cultivation

Financial value of Government contribution to those 50 farmers amounts to Rs. 700,000 each under which required planting material, drip irrigation systems, land preparation and technological advises will be given to those farmers. The supporting institution is the Agriculture Sector Modernization Project. Farmers of the project have stated that they receive an income of Rs. 80,000 - 10,000 per week. Higher demand is available for passion fruit both in the local market and the foreign market.

Issuing rice to all supermarkets

Arrangements have been made to issue rice to all super markets, Sathosa sale outlets (C.W.E.) and co-operative sale centres from 1st April at Government controlled price in view of the coming festive season. Accordingly consumers may purchase ‘’Nadu’’ at Rs.97 and Red and White ‘’Kekulu’’ at Rs.95/- Chairman, CWE, Co-operative Commissioner and Chief Executive Officers of all Parties
