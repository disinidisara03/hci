Pesticide Control Act

The Act

An act to provide for the licensing of pesticides; to regulate the import, packing, labelling, storage, formulation, transport, sale and use thereof; for the appointment of a licensing authority for pesticides; for the establishment of a pesticide formulary commitee and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.

Regulations

  • These Regulations may be cited as Control of Pesticides (Pest Control Services) Regulations No. 01 of 20105.
  • No “Pest Control Service” shall conduct pest control activities without a license issued by the Registrar.

Regulations continued..

  • Every application for a license to carry out pest control activities shall be in the prescribed form and shall be accompanied by a fee of Rupees Twenty Thousand.
  • The Registrar shall on receipt of an application and with the fee submit the application to the committee for its approval.
