Research

The Drive to Produce More, with Less

Increasing global population with limited land for farming, is creating an urgent need to improve farm productivity in a sustainable way. Our customers are leading the charge through agricultural research and innovation

  • Higher yield seed varieties
  • Fertilisers
  • Soil microbes

Study

Globally agriculture will need to feed over nine billion people by 2050. With a changing climate and increasing competition for land, water and energy resources, the agricultural sector will need to find innovative ways to do more with less.

Future

Researchers continue to make new and sometimes surprising discoveries that advance the field. As we innovate, we must be diligent to examine the durability of new research findings over time and across contexts.
