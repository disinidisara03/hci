Training Division

Training

Training Department of Export Agriculture is a technical Department which produces new technologies for improvement of traditional cultivation, harvesting and processing practices of Export Agriculture Crops. To transfer new technologies to stakeholders of the sector training activities have been organized in a broad spectrum. Training programs of the department include In-service training, Farmer training, and Cinnamon peeler training
In-Service training center of the department is located at Elwala, Matale. The center has residential facilities and equipped with all learning facilities.

For Whom The Training Applies To

In-service training programs are conducted for Farmer Groups, Community Based Organizations, NGOs, groups of Entrepreneurs, Other public/semi-public Institutes or Departments who are interested in gaining knowledge on cultivation, processing, and marketing of EAC such as Cinnamon, Pepper, Coffee, Cocoa etc.

Nature of Training

Training programs are conducted at the In-Service Training Centre located at Elwala, Matale. Programs are essentially residential. Duration varies from 2-5 days. Lectures are conducted by training officers of the ISTC, Reseach Officers, Assistant Directors, and external resource persons.
Lectures will follow practical sessions in cultivation plots near to ISTC. Field visits are also arranged according to the necessities of programs. Food and lodging are provided to the trainees.
