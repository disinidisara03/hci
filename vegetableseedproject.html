ABOUT THE VEGETABLE SEED PROJECT

ENHANCING THE PRODUCTION SYSTEM OF CERTIFIED VEGETABLE SEEDS IN SRI LANKA!

In the aim to improve a current self-sufficient rate of Other Field Crops (OFC) and vegetables in Sri Lanka, the Government of Sri Lanka has put it's priority towards increasing the food production. Also, Government of Sri Lanka set its own target as doubling production of certified vegetable seeds through improving productivity and quality of domestic vegetable seed production under public-private partnership initiatives. In these circumstances, Government of Sri Lanka requested a technical cooperation project to the government of Japan through Japan International Cooperation Agency, aiming to improve agricultural productivity and quality through development and disseminating applicable production technology for farmers of qualified vegetable seeds. The Project is implemented in the framework of Japan International Cooperation Agency which main tackling issues are low productivity, lack of agricultural management technology of farmers, diversification of agricultural products, participatory development. This project aims to tackle the issue of agricultural productivity and diversification of agriculture and have high validity in Japan cooperation strategy. The availability of certified vegetable seed in the country will be increased.
